# Product Overview

GramSetu (Village Bridge) is a voice-first, multilingual AI agent platform designed to bridge the information and literacy gap for rural farmers in India. The system enables farmers to access government scheme information, market intelligence, and weather data through natural voice conversations in their native languages.

## Core Value Proposition

Democratizes access to government schemes and financial services by eliminating language and digital literacy barriers through voice-first interaction.

## Target Users

Rural farmers in India with limited digital literacy, primarily interacting in Hindi, Hinglish, Telugu, or Tamil.

## Key Features

- **Voice-First Interaction**: Natural language conversations in native languages (Hindi, Hinglish, Telugu, Tamil)
- **Government Scheme Discovery**: RAG-based knowledge base for scheme information, eligibility, and application processes
- **Voice-to-Form Filling**: Hero feature that converts spoken farmer details into ready-to-submit PDF applications (starting with Kisan Credit Card)
- **Market Intelligence**: Current mandi prices and actionable advice for crop selling decisions
- **Weather Advisory**: Location-based weather forecasts integrated with farming advice
- **Progressive Authentication**: Guest mode for information queries, phone OTP for applications

## MVP Scope

- Hindi/Hinglish + one regional language (Telugu or Tamil)
- Kisan Credit Card (KCC) application as primary form-filling use case
- Mobile-first PWA with WhatsApp-style interface
- AWS-based architecture (Transcribe, Bedrock, Polly, Knowledge Bases)
